Early years in the states

I was 8 years old when I first came to the state. My dad had work in a restaurent in Tenessse and that is where I spent the first couple of years.It was a very small town with about just 1000 people population. Very quite little place. Till this day, I can remember where everything is located. There was only one Kroger, only one Walmart, only one bank(Bank of America), only one McDonald, and many many more onlys.

Most fun year of teen years

After the three years pass, we moved to NYC due to my dads work again.

NYC was the first big city that I have ever lived in. It was shocking for a 12 year old. Everything felt so new and interesting. I met a lot of kids there that were just like me. They were raised in China, then they came to the State in their teens. I felt so connect to them. We share similar mindsets, had similar interests, and for the first time, I felt like I had some real friend.

Happy times never last long. I only had one year in NYC and had to move again for my dads work reason again. It wasn't fun. I remeber crying and all that. I didn't want to leave. I had all my friends there, all my favorite shops, restaurent and everything will get left behind. It was a really dark time for me.

And here we are

After that, we moved here to Lexington. I have been here for 9 years now. I still can't belive that its been 9 whole years. It still feels like a dream. In this 9 years, I met some new friends, had new favorite restaurent. It is also this city that gave me my first job, first car, first computer, and many many more. I have traveled back to NYC a couple of times within that 9 years. I gotta say, lately, it feeling more and more like it is more fun here and that moving here might have been a right call. With all been said, I am still living here and hoping for each days to only get better.
